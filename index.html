03 · PROTOCOL

How the forge works.

01
Browse
Every prompt submitted by the community is visible the moment it's added — no review queue.
02
Forge
Hit add_prompt. Drop your title, body, category, tags. It deploys to the live vault in real-time.
03
Sync
Every visitor sees your prompt instantly via Firestore's realtime channel. No refresh, no delay.
04
Deploy
Tap copy. Paste into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or your tool of choice. Sharpen, iterate, share back.

Built for operators.

PROMPT//FORGE is an open, community-driven prompt library. No paywalls, no curation gatekeepers. The best prompts win by being copied. Inspired by hacker terminal aesthetics. Built with a real-time backend so the arsenal grows in front of your eyes.
