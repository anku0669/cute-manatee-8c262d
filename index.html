How it works.

Four steps. No accounts. No friction.

01

Browse

Every prompt is visible the moment it's added. There's no review queue — the community curates by what gets copied.

02

Forge

Drop a title, category, tags, and the prompt body. It deploys to the live vault in a single tap.

03

Sync

Every visitor sees your prompt instantly via Firestore's realtime channel. No refresh, no delay.

04

Deploy

Copy, paste into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or your tool of choice. Sharpen, iterate, share back.
